Press [A] to go left, [D] to right, [SPACE] to jump,
[F] to shot, [Q] to switch weapon, [R] to reload.

Debug section:

Press <F2> to show collision and sprite boxes.

Press <F4> to spawn slime.

Trivia:
Revolver deals 30 dmg, AK47 only 15.
Green slime have 10hp and 10 attack, jumps infrequent.
Blue slime have 30hp and 15 attack.
Red slime have 50hp and 20 attack and jump very frequent! Restores 10hp after killing him.
Be careful! Falling from a great height deals damage to player!
